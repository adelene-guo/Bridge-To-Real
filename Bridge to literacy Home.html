BRIDGE TO LITERACY

Building bridges to a brighter future through literacy








Bridge to Literacy is an international, student-led nonprofit that fosters a love of language through English, the most globally spoken language, to encourage students to pursue opportunities in the real world such as work and traveling. We teach children and teens from underserved communities around the world in Kenya, Honduras, Ukraine, Mexico, Gaza, and more. A widely recognized organization, we provide students with access to greater opportunities while fostering meaningful relationships that transcend borders.
