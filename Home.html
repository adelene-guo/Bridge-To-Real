BRIDGE TO LITERACY

Building bridges to a brighter future through literacy







Versatile as well as vibrant, Bridge to Literacy is committed to educational equity by delivering asynchronous volunteer-created video lessons in addition to live lessons for our partners in Kenya and China to ensure that every child can have the Bridge to Literacy experience - no matter their Wifi connectivity. Unlike traditional schooling, which forces children to sit through forty minutes of lectures, the Bridge to Literacy education experience dismantles the teacher-student hierarchy, encouraging children to take the reins in their language-learning experiences through lessons that involve physically pointing to objects, walking around the room to learn, not just grammar or vocabulary, but comprehension, public speaking skills, and cross-cultural respect that enables them to flourish not only in academics, but in their personal and professional lives.

We are proud to be funded by the U.S. Department of State via the University of Iowa. Funding for Bridge to Literacy has been provided by the U.S. Department of State’s Bureau of Education and Cultural Affairs via the International Writing Program and the University of Iowa as the implementing partners.






Bridge to Literacy is a proud recipient of the Hershey Heart Warming Action Grant, a monetary award provided by the Hershey Foundation in partnership with the Heart Warming Project to support youth projects that spark connection and create community where they live.
The President's Volunteer Service Award is a civil award bestowed by the President of the United States and established to honor volunteers that give hundreds of hours per year helping others. All volunteers of Bridge to Literacy gain the opportunity of earning this award.




Bridge to Literacy is honored to recieve the Taco Bell Foundation Ambition Accelerator Scholarship which is given to accelerate young innovators’ most ambitious social impact ideas.
